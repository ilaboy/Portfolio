Hurricane Maria

Hurricane Maria was one of the most dangerous and deadliest hurricanes of 2017 hurricane season in the
North Atlantic. Maria devastated Puerto Rico, Dominica, and U.S. Virgin Islands. The map below displays
the path taken by Hurricane Maria, from its ealy stages as a Tropical Depresion and Tropical Storm up to
its final stages as an Extratropical Cyclone. The hurricane icons shows the date and storm strength of the
storm at that particular location. This hurricane data was taken from the National Hurricane Center and
Central Pacific Hurricane Center.
